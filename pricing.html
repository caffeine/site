Pricing and Billing FAQ


How much is Caffeine?

$500/month for iOS apps with less than 5k MAU. Custom enterprise pricing above that. Please contact us to sign up.


When will Caffeine be ready?

Caffeine is currently transitioning from closed beta to public production. If you have signed up for our waitlist you should receive an invite soon. If you have not, please contact us for a trial.


What about supporting services?

All pricing plans include 24/7 technical support provided by our engineers, weekly performance reports, and A/B testing functionality.


Am I charged for uncaffeinated network requests?

We don't bill on a per-request basis.


Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

You can upgrade or cancel at anytime.


Can I run caffeine on my own server?

Please contact us to register your interest in self-hosting Caffeine. We are very interested in keeping your data on-premises and how we can build a self-host product that works for you.


How do I cancel my account?

You can cancel your account anytime through by emailing your account representative or support.


Do you offer a guarantee?

Yes. If we don't speed up a request in your application by at least 50%, we will completely refund you.






Still have more questions?

Contact us or shoot us an email at help@caffei.net
