What is Caffeine?

3 years of R&D by frustrated iOS developers— a purely software solution that speeds the hell out of your iOS network requests.

Hassle Free

Caffeine works out of the box, and integration takes 15 minutes. Get back to building features, stop worrying about low level performance optimizations.

Blistering Speed

Caffeine is faster than your CDN and works even when other tools like caching and compression can't. User login, API requests, uploads, and even loading fresh data on launch are all accelerated by our tech.

Just iOS

Current solutions are jack of all trades, master of none. Caffeine is laser-focused on iOS performance and we say no to anything that slows us down.

Fully Secure

Caffeine protects your data using NaCl, the peer-reviewed cryptographic library written for next-generation applications. Each request is protected with 256-bit elliptical curve cryptography with PFS.
