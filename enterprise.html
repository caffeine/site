Security

Ensure secure transmission of data



Security isn't just something you bolt on, it's fundamental to the technology design. Caffeine traffic is protected by NaCl, the peer-reviewed cryptography technology. We use 256-bit elliptical curve technology to lock down all your data. Are you a cryptographer? Then you'll want to know we use a CurveCP-derived key exchange to generate unique Curve25519 per-session keys, achieving perfect forward secrecy for every session. Requests and responses are protected with the well-studied salsa20poly1305, a construction you can read about in the NaCl paper. Non-cryptographer? Our technology is more secure than what you're doing now.
